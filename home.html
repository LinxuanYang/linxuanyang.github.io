Hi, everyone! This is my dog rosa who was born on June 19, 2017. She is a adorable girl.

Something About Rosa's Name

Rosa has a very long name which is much longer than other puppies. While people like to name their dogs kiki, coco, daifuku, we named our dog Clearvu Rosamonde of Waltham which comes from the strict name rule of her breading line. Clearvu is the name of dog kennels and they required that every puppy's first name from this line should named after a father or mother name of a country. So, we picked Rosamonde. Lastly, there should be the name of the city where we live. Since we live in Waltham, a city of great Boston area, we finally named our puppy Clearvu Rosamonde of Waltham. Click here for more information about Clearvu kennels.

What Does Rosa Eat

As a puppy, we feed Rosa 3 times a day with a cup of puppy food per time. When she grow up, we switched to 3 times a day with two cups of puppy food. About on eighth month, Rosa started to eat 3 cups perday because we though she was a little bit fatty. However, the vet said she needed more weight, so we switched to 6 cups per day again. Overall, we reduce her food when she seems too fat or increase food when she seems too thin.
