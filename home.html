Something About Rosa's Name

Rosa has a very long name which is much longer than other puppies. While people like to name their dogs like Kiki, Coco, Daifuku, we named our dog Clearvu Rosamonde of Waltham which came from the strict name rule of her breading line. Clearvu is the name of the dog kennels and they required that every puppy's first name from this line should named after a famous man or woman. So, we picked Rosamonde. Lastly, there should be the name of the city where we live after last name and first name. Since I live in Waltham, a city of great Boston area, we finally named this puppy Clearvu Rosamonde of Waltham. Learn more > about information of Clearvu kennels if you are interested in raising a puppy.

What Does Rosa Eat

As a puppy, we feed Rosa 3 times a day with a cup of puppy food per time. When she grow up, we switched to 3 times a day with two cups of puppy food. About on eighth month, Rosa started to eat 3 cups perday because we though she was a little bit fatty. However, the vet said she needed more weight, so we switched to 6 cups per day again. Overall, we reduce her food when she seems too fat or increase food when she seems too thin.
